1. Introduction

Welcome to Scroll Sense ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and disclosure when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using Scroll Sense, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Password: Stored securely using Firebase Authentication (encrypted, not accessible to us)
  • Account Creation Date: Timestamp of when you created your account
  • Last Login Date: Timestamp of your most recent login

2.2 Usage and Activity Data

We collect data about how you use Scroll Sense:

  • Social Media Usage Data: Time spent on social media across 9 content categories (Beauty & Body Image, Connecting with Friends, Entertainment, Trends & Challenges, Mean Content, Educational Content, Celebrity Content, Gaming, Posting Own Content)
  • Daily Check-in Data: Your daily social media usage patterns, mood ratings, and optional notes
  • Mood Tracking Data: Mood ratings (1-5 scale), mood comparisons, and mood-related notes
  • Journal Entries: Written reflections, mood ratings, tags, and privacy settings for each entry
  • Goal Data: Wellness goals you set, progress tracking, and completion status
  • Sleep Health Data: Social media usage before bedtime and sleep quality correlations

2.3 Content Analysis Data

When you use our AI-powered content analysis feature:

  • Social Media Content: Text or screenshots you submit for analysis
  • Analysis Results: Emotional impact scores, content categorization, and recommendations
  • Note: Content you analyze is sent to third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude and OpenAI) for processing. We do not store the content you analyze - it is only used for the analysis and then discarded.

2.4 App Usage Data

We collect information about your interaction with the App:

  • Feature Usage: Which features you use and how often
  • App Performance Data: Crash reports, error logs, and performance metrics (collected automatically)
  • Device Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version (for troubleshooting)

2.5 Optional Data

You may optionally provide:

  • Display Name: A name you choose to display in your profile
  • Profile Photo: If you choose to upload one
  • Custom Mindfulness Messages: Personal reminders you create

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 App Functionality

  • To create and manage your account
  • To authenticate and verify your identity
  • To provide core features: mood tracking, journaling, content analysis, goal setting
  • To generate insights, charts, and visualizations based on your data
  • To send verification codes and account-related emails

3.2 Content Analysis

  • To analyze social media content you submit using AI services
  • To provide emotional impact scores and recommendations
  • To help you understand how content affects your mental well-being

3.3 App Improvement

  • To understand how users interact with the App
  • To identify and fix bugs or technical issues
  • To improve App performance and user experience
  • To develop new features based on usage patterns

3.4 Communication

  • To send you verification codes for account security
  • To respond to your support requests
  • To send important updates about the App (if you opt in)

4.2 SendGrid (Twilio)

  • Purpose: Sending verification code emails and account-related emails

  • Privacy Policy: SendGrid Privacy Policy

  • Privacy Policy: Anthropic Privacy Policy

4.4 OpenAI API

  • Purpose: AI-powered content analysis (alternative to Anthropic)
  • Data Shared: Social media content you submit for analysis
  • Important: Content is sent to OpenAI for analysis but is NOT stored by OpenAI or us after analysis completes
  • Privacy Policy: OpenAI Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

  • Cloud Storage: Your data is stored securely on Firebase/Firestore servers (Google Cloud infrastructure)
  • Local Storage: Some app settings and preferences are stored locally on your device
  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication: Passwords are hashed and encrypted by Firebase Authentication - we cannot see your password

5.2 Security Measures

  • Firebase security rules restrict access to your data - only you can access your own data
  • Authentication tokens are securely managed
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Secure API connections for all third-party services

5.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage Data: Retained to provide historical insights and trends
  • Journal Entries: Retained until you delete them or delete your account
  • Content Analysis: Content you analyze is NOT stored - only analysis results are saved
  • Deleted Accounts: All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
