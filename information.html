❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between Goals and Plan Your Day? +

Goals are behavioral targets (e.g., "reduce Instagram to 1 hour"). Plan Your Day is a timeline (e.g., "9-9:30 am study, 3-4 pm break with friends"). Goals track progress; plans structure your day. Use both together for maximum impact.

How do I enable app blocking? +

Go to Settings → App Blocking → Enable Screen Time/Family Controls permission (required by iOS) → Turn on Focus Shields → Select which apps to block by context (Default, Sleep, Goals, Pomodoro, Mindful).

Can I export my data? +

Yes! Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data. Your data exports as a JSON file with all entries, goals, and statistics. Share it via email or keep it for your records.

How does the AI analysis work? +

Paste social media text or upload a screenshot. Scroll Sense sends it to Anthropic Claude or OpenAI for analysis. You get back emotional impact scores, category classification, and wellness recommendations. Content is NOT stored after analysis.

What if app blocking seems off? +

Check that Screen Time/Family Controls permissions are enabled in iOS Settings → Screen Time. Also verify your blocking lists and schedules are active. Some background system behaviors depend on iOS.

Can I delete my account and data? +

Yes. Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This is permanent—all your data will be deleted within 30 days. You can also email support@scrollsenseapp.com for additional data deletion assistance.
