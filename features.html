Dashboard & Analytics

See your digital wellness progress at a glance with interactive dashboards, Scroll Score, mood trends, and category breakdowns.

  • Composite wellness score (Scroll Score)
  • Interactive mood vs. time charts
  • Category breakdowns with stacked bars
  • Weekly and monthly trend analysis
  • Visual correlations between usage and mood
  • Progress tracking across all metrics

Daily Check-In

Log your daily social media usage across 6 categories and track how different content types affect your mood and well-being.

  • Track time across 6 content categories
  • Log mood with each check-in
  • Add personal notes and reflections
  • Create multiple check-ins per day
  • Calendar view of history
  • Pattern recognition over time

Mindfulness Tools

Take mindful breaks with guided breathing exercises, customizable reminders, and wellness prompts.

  • Guided breathing exercises
  • Customizable reminder intervals
  • Personal reminder customization
  • Full-screen mindfulness overlays
  • Countdown timers for next reminder
  • Usage-threshold auto-nudges

Goal Setting

Create personalized wellness goals with app blocking support and real-time progress tracking.

  • Normal, timing, and social-media style goals
  • Duration and date window configuration
  • Per-goal app blocking
  • Satisfaction rating on completion
  • Suggested and custom goals
  • Progress statistics and trends

Reflection Journal

Build self-awareness through daily writing, track mood patterns, and maintain consistency with streaks.

  • Create titled journal entries
  • Add tags for organization
  • Mood tracking with each entry
  • Privacy settings (private by default)
  • Streak tracking and motivation
  • Historical mood trend visualization

Sleep Health

  • Bedtime and wake time logging
  • Sleep quality rating

  • Weekday/weekend configuration
  • Sleep trend analytics

Create practical daily schedules with time blocks, priorities, and reminders that actually work.

Convert productivity goals into repeatable focus intervals with custom work/break cycles and app blocking.

  • Classic Pomodoro timing

App Blocking (Focus Shields)

Robust app blocking with context-aware lists and clear notifications of why apps are restricted.

  • Context-specific blocking lists

  • Scheduled blocking windows
  • Dynamic reason tracking
  • iOS Screen Time integration
