Key Research Findings

📉

Reduced Daily Social Media Usage

After only 30 days of using Scroll Sense, approximately 60% of participants reduced their daily social media usage.

⏳

Less Time Spent Scrolling

84% of participants reported spending less time on social media, including reducing late-night scrolling habits.

🧠

More Intentional Social Media Use

90% of participants became more intentional about their social media habits, replacing mindless scrolling with purposeful use.

🎯

Improved Focus During Tasks

88% of participants reported that Scroll Sense improved their focus during studying and important tasks.

😊

Improved Feelings After Social Media

78% of participants reported feeling less stressed, less drained, or more positive after using social media with Scroll Sense.

😌

Reduced Anxiety After Social Media

Anxiety after social media use decreased from approximately 74% to 45% after participants used Scroll Sense, showing a significant improvement in emotional well-being.

🌙

Improved Sleep & Reduced Distractions

Participants experienced significant improvements in sleep habits and fewer distractions during schoolwork after only one month of using Scroll Sense.

🚫

Reduced Habitual Scrolling

84% of participants agreed that Scroll Sense helped reduce opening social media out of habit or boredom and helped them avoid distractions during important tasks.

📚

Healthier Digital Habits

Participants shifted toward healthier social media behaviors, increasing educational use and meaningful connections while reducing mindless scrolling.

🚀

Greatest Improvements Among Highest Users

Participants who initially struggled the most with excessive social media use experienced the largest reductions in screen time and improvements in well-being.

Research Impact

The results demonstrate that Scroll Sense can help teens become more aware of their digital habits, reduce unhealthy usage patterns, improve focus, and build a healthier relationship with technology through reflection, goal-setting, and mindfulness.
