About Scroll Sense

We're on a mission to help people build healthier relationships with technology

To empower people—especially teens and young adults—to take control of their digital wellness by providing evidence-based tools, support, and community that transform social media use from mindless scrolling into intentional, purposeful engagement.

Our Story

The Problem We Saw

Social media was designed to be addictive. Algorithms optimize for engagement at the expense of user well-being, and most people felt powerless to change their habits. Anxiety, sleep disruption, and reduced focus were common complaints—especially among teens.

Existing solutions were either too punitive (complete blocking) or ineffective (generic productivity apps). We needed something that treated the root cause: helping people become intentional and aware of their choices.

🚀

Building the Solution

We combined behavioral psychology, digital wellness research, and user feedback to create Scroll Sense—a comprehensive platform that makes it easy to:

  • Track your habits and understand patterns
  • Plan your day with intention
  • Take mindful breaks
  • Protect your focus and sleep
  • Reflect and build awareness
  • Stay motivated with gamification

Research-Backed Results

60% of users reduced daily social media usage

With clinically meaningful improvements in anxiety, focus, sleep quality, and emotional well-being.

Statistical significance: p = 1.4 × 10⁻⁶

These aren't random fluctuations—they're real, measurable, scientifically validated improvements.

Heavy users benefit most

Those most addicted to social media experienced the greatest benefits—exactly who needs help the most.
