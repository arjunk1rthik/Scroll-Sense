Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Scroll Sense

🚀 Getting Started

What is Scroll Sense? +

Scroll Sense is a digital wellness app that helps you reduce mindless scrolling and build healthier social media habits. It combines planning, reflection, mood tracking, app blocking, gamification, and AI analysis to help you use social media more intentionally.

Who is Scroll Sense for? +

Scroll Sense is designed for anyone struggling with social media overuse—teens, students, professionals, and parents. The app is particularly effective for people dealing with FOMO, anxiety, sleep issues, productivity challenges, or simply wanting a healthier relationship with technology.

Do I need an account? +

You can try Scroll Sense in guest mode without an account. However, creating an account (with email and password) gives you cloud sync, persistent data storage, and access to all features. Account creation is free and takes less than 30 seconds.

How much does Scroll Sense cost? +

Scroll Sense is available on the App Store. Check the App Store listing for current pricing and any subscription options.

⚙️ Features & Usage

What's the best way to use Scroll Sense? +

Recommended daily routine:

  • Morning (5 min): Set your daily goals for each social media category to create intention
  • Throughout day: Use mindfulness reminders, app blocking, and stay aware of your usage
  • Evening (10 min): Complete your daily check-in, log your actual usage, and journal about your experience

Consistency is key—doing this every day multiplies the benefits and builds lasting habits.

How does app blocking work? +

Scroll Sense integrates with iOS Screen Time to block distracting apps during your defined focus periods. You can set different blocking lists for different contexts:

  • Default: Always-blocked apps
  • Sleep: Bedtime blocking to protect rest
  • Goals: Per-goal specific blocking
  • Pomodoro: Blocked during focus sessions
  • Mindful: Blocked during meditation

Note: You'll need to enable Screen Time/Family Controls permission in iOS Settings.

Can I customize reminders and notifications? +

Yes! Go to Settings and configure:

  • Mindfulness reminder intervals
  • Sleep notification timing
  • Goal check-in reminders
  • Daily check-in notifications
  • Streak risk alerts

You can also turn notifications off completely if preferred, or disable them in iOS Settings → Notifications.

How does the AI content analysis feature work? +

The AI analysis feature uses Anthropic's Claude or OpenAI to analyze social media content you submit:

  • Paste text or upload a screenshot
  • The AI analyzes emotional impact, categorization, and wellness implications
  • You get insights about how the content might affect your mood
  • Your content is NOT stored—it's deleted after analysis
  • This is completely optional and you can skip it entirely
What's the difference between Goals and Plan Your Day? +

Goals are behavioral targets you want to achieve (e.g., "reduce Instagram to 1 hour/day"). Plan Your Day is a practical timeline that structures your day into time blocks (e.g., "9-9:30 study, 3-4 friend time, 7-8 dinner").

Use them together: Goals define what you want to accomplish, and Plans help you actually execute those goals throughout your day.

🔒 Data & Privacy

Is my data private? +

Yes. Your data is encrypted, stored securely on Firebase/Google Cloud, and only accessible to you with your password. Journal entries are private by default. We don't sell your data or use it for advertising.

For detailed privacy information, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I export my data? +

Yes! Go to Settings → Data & Privacy → Export Data. Your data exports as a JSON file containing all journal entries, goals, check-ins, achievements, and settings. You can save it or share it as needed.

How do I delete my account? +

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Confirm the action, and all your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

You can also email support@scrollsenseapp.com to request deletion assistance.

Is my password stored securely? +

Your password is handled by Firebase Authentication, which uses industry-standard encryption. We never see your password—it's hashed and encrypted. Even Scroll Sense team members cannot access your password.

What about content I analyze with AI? +

When you use the AI analysis feature, your content is sent to Anthropic or OpenAI for processing. Your content is NOT stored by us or the AI services after analysis. It's analyzed and then discarded. This is noted in our Privacy Policy.

🔧 Troubleshooting

App blocking isn't working. What do I do? +

Check these things:

  • Is Screen Time enabled in iOS Settings → Screen Time?
  • Did you grant Scroll Sense Screen Time/Family Controls permission?
  • Are your blocking lists configured in Settings → App Blocking?
  • Is Focus Shields toggled ON?
  • Check your schedule is set to the current day of the week

If it's still not working, try force-closing the app and reopening it. Contact support if the issue persists.

I forgot my password. How do I reset it? +

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email. You'll receive a password reset link via email. Follow the link to create a new password.

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support@scrollsenseapp.com.

My data isn't syncing across devices +

Make sure you're signed into the same account on both devices. Cloud sync requires an active internet connection. Try:

  • Sign out and sign back in
  • Check your internet connection
  • Force close and reopen the app
  • Update to the latest app version

Contact support if sync issues continue.

The app keeps crashing. What should I do? +

Try these steps:

  • Update to the latest iOS version
  • Update to the latest Scroll Sense version
  • Force close the app (swipe up from bottom, hold, then close)
  • Restart your device
  • Clear app cache (iOS Settings → General → Storage)

If crashes persist, email support@scrollsenseapp.com with details about when it crashes.

👤 Account & Subscription

Can I use Scroll Sense on multiple devices? +

Yes! Sign into the same account on multiple iOS devices and your data will sync across them. Guest mode data won't sync, so we recommend creating an account for the best experience.

Can I download Scroll Sense on Android? +

Currently, Scroll Sense is available on iOS. Check the App Store for the latest updates on platform availability.

Do you offer family accounts or group plans? +

Each person needs their own individual account. However, you can share your daily plans and progress with family members via the sharing feature to maintain accountability.

❤️ Health & Safety

Is Scroll Sense a medical device or therapy tool? +

No. Scroll Sense is a wellness app for self-awareness and habit building, not a medical device or substitute for professional mental health care. If you're experiencing serious mental health concerns, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

Where can I find mental health resources? +

If you're in crisis, contact emergency services immediately. Scroll Sense has quick-access crisis resources in Settings. You can also reach out to:

  • National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988
  • Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357
